Simply Guacamole

Due to it's limited ingredients and low prep time required, there shouldn't be anything stopping you from trying this killer guacamole!

Ingredients

  • 5 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ¾ cup minced green onion
  • ½ cup minced fresh cilantro
  • salt and pepper, to taste
